Gold
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Gold (g) is an essential resource in The Pit. Similarly to XP, accumulating it is required to prestige.
The following are some ways players obtain gold and increase their gold-earning rate:
  • Killing players with a bounty will award the killer with the bounty.
  • Gold Boost is an upgrade purchasable at level 10. Each tier grants an additional +10% more gold from kills and ingots.
  • Conglomerate is a perk unlocked in the Renown Shop. It removes the ability to earn XP, transmuting every XP earned into 0.20g.
  • The Highlander Megastreak grants the user +110% gold from all kills and the ability to earn one's bounty after dying. It is a Renown upgrade available at prestige VII.
  • Renown upgrades such as Renown Gold Boost, Celebrity, and Co-op Cat are extremely useful for increasing a player's gold output.
In order to prestige, a player needs to have grinded a certain amount of gold. This does not necessarily mean that the player needs to have that amount of gold in their inventory; the gold needs to have been earned at any point during the prestige.
Actions such as trading do not count towards the gold requirement.
