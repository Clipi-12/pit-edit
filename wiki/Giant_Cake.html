Giant Cake
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Giant Cake is a minor event occuring in the Pit. The event lasts for 2 minutes.
When the event occurs, a giant cake, created by hundreds of smaller cakes in a cuboid shape, spawns in a random area of the map. Players are able to eat slices of the cake by right-clicking it, similar to vanilla Minecraft. However, by eating slices of the cake, players will receive gold. Most cake slices award 1 gold. This increases by 0.25 gold for every 50 cake slices eaten. Eating the last slice of a cake awards 5 gold, increasing by 1.25g for every 50 slices eaten.
Two additional types of cake exist in the giant cake:
Cherries, which award 150 gold, increasing by 37.5g for every 50 slices eaten,
Chocolate Chips, which award 100 XP. The XP earned from chocolate chips is unaffected by XP Boost.

Trivia
Giant Cake was added in The Pit 0.4 - Events Extravaganza. Five players were credited to its creation; players suggested names such as "Baker's Dream" or "Cake Hunt" for the event.
Earning 5,000g or more from a single Giant Cake event grants the player with the "Sugar Rush" achievement, rewarding 5 Achievement Points.
