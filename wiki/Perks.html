Perks
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Perks are upgrades players can purchase to get minor buffs during combat.
Players can purchase perks starting at level 10. Additional perk slots are unlocked at levels 30 and 70, with a fourth perk slot being purchaseable at level 100 with the Extra Perk Slot Renown upgrade unlocked at prestige III.
